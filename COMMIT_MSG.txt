Bilancio: bolder tabs, sub-tabs, stronger totals, SP/CE tints

Styling only (no logic changed), to make the balance sheet less sterile.
- Stato Patrimoniale / Conto Economico main tabs are bolder and larger, with a
  section-coloured active underline.
- Attivita/Passivita and Costi/Ricavi are now sub-tabs (nav-tabs) instead of pills.
- Totals summary cards are more prominent (shadow + coloured side accent).
- Two light, distinct tints applied to both the totals boxes and the grid below:
  Stato Patrimoniale = slate blue, Conto Economico = sage green (via .card-tint-*
  classes and a tint param on the sezioneTree/sezioneTreeCompact fragments).

Version 0.13.1. Sandbox checks only.
