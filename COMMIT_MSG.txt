Add unified documents dashboard with traceability (/documenti)

Port of MENU_RISTAMPA_DOC ("Ristampa documenti"), the most-used module of
the legacy client: one dashboard to consult ALL document archives, plus
the ordine <-> DDT <-> fattura chain. Read-only: physical reprint (.frx),
e-mail and FE generation stay on the VFP client. Depends on the previous
4 patches (applies last in the chain).

- DocumentArchiveDao: port of the legacy dynamic archive selection
  (&_V_ARCH_TT). NamedParameterJdbcTemplate on tenantDataSource (same
  pattern as WarehouseValuationDao); table names only from the
  DocumentType enum (controlled identifiers); OFFSET/FETCH pagination.
  Causale via PARA 'TOP'+ORD_CAUS -> DESCRI, agente via 'AGE'+ORD_AGE
  (verbatim from the legacy query).
- Type catalog (9 verified): ORD, OFF, ORF, BOL, BFO, RDC (ord_tipo=9),
  FAP, FAT, INR. Excluded and tracked: RFI/ORT/REC/REF/BOF/BCL (no
  discriminator in sources, NEEDS_DOMAIN); FAF (U_FAF_TT absent at
  Impresind, NOT_APPLICABLE).
- Traceability card "Documenti collegati" for the sales chain,
  reconstructed from U_BOL_DD refs (ORS_NUMORC/ORS_DATORC for the order,
  MOV_NUMFAT/MOV_DATFAT for the invoice), with resolved clickable links.
- Domain finding + DDT FIX: the Ristampa grid binds ORD_NUMORD/ORD_DATORD
  as Numero/Data for ALL types -> on DDT rows the linked customer order is
  the ORC pair, and ORS_NUMORD is the header self-reference. DdtHead/
  DdtRow linked-order mappings corrected (ORD_NUMORC/ORD_DATORC and
  ORS_NUMORC/ORS_DATORC); internal number exposed separately.
- Dashboard UI: legacy-style filter panel (tipo, anno, periodo, numeri,
  soggetto, riferimento, articolo via taggancio subquery); local sortable
  headers and pager preserving ALL filters (shared fragments untouched).
- MenuService FORM_TO_URL: menu_ristampa_doc -> /documenti (tbl_menu leaf
  'gestioneristampadocumenti', reachable from L1 clienti AND fornitori).
- Migration tracker: 9 rows for MENU_RISTAMPA_DOC.SCX.

Verification (sandbox only): every SQL column checked against the schema
CSV for ALL 8 TT + 8 DD archives (none missing); brace balance OK; div
balance OK; CSV 12 columns/row; full 5-patch chain applies cleanly on
fresh clone of HEAD d4652ec. To confirm at first deploy: Maven build,
TAGGANCIO population on historical data, OFFSET/FETCH performance on the
largest archives, RDC ord_tipo=9 on real data.
