Add accounting consultation (read-only): 4 areas

First accounting slice, all read-only on legacy tables:
- Primanota: registration list + double-entry detail (Dare/Avere) with totals
  and an out-of-balance warning.
- Storico contabile: per-account ledger (mastrino) with a running balance.
- Bilancio: totals Dare/Avere and balance per account, with KPI cards and
  links into the ledger.
- Partitari clienti e fornitori: open items with residual (total - paid).

Model reads MOV_CONT (movements, MOV_TMOV = 'D'/'A', grouped by MOV_NREGIS),
PART_CLI and PART_FOR. Only schema-verified columns are mapped; every entity
is strictly read-only. Amounts and running balances are computed in Java;
account descriptions are resolved from CONTI. Year and society come from the
existing FiscalContext (the interceptor already guarantees it is set).

Data entry for primanota (writing to MOV_CONT) is intentionally left to a
separate slice, since writing to legacy tables needs a plug&play-safe design.

Rebased on top of the supplier-registry patch (shared files MenuService.java
and CLAUDE.md merged). Verification: applies clean on a fresh clone with the
supplier patch already applied.
