Add IVA statistics from precomputed period totals

Part 2 of 2 - apply AFTER the registers patch. Read-only statistics at
/contabilita/registri-iva/statistiche from U_IVA_TO (period totals
written by every legacy bollati load; grain soc+anno+mese+codiva+
clifor+fatnot).

- Filters: lato (vendite = CLIFOR C,D; acquisti = F,A; cee = E,R;
  tutti) and month.
- Per-month and per-IVA-code aggregations. Fatture (F), note di
  accredito (N) and corrispettivi (C) are kept as SEPARATE rows in both
  views: the netting conventions (F-N) belong to the liquidazione -
  fiscal logic reserved to Opus - so the viewer deliberately does not
  compensate between types.
- Raw-grain <details> table for transparency, with decoded lato labels.
- Nav pills wired both ways with the three register views.

Verification (sandbox only): checks as in part 1; chain verified on
fresh clone of HEAD 0dfb86b.
