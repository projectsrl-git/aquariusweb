SEMANTICS (appended to cespiti/README.md with file:line evidence,
from ALLINEA_CESPITI_QUOTE_AMM.PRG + MENU_quo_am_gen/simu.SCX):
- QUO_VALORD/ANT/ACC = quota DELL'ESERCIZIO per channel;
  QUO_FONORD/ANT/ACC = fondo PROGRESSIVO a fine esercizio
  (gen: _X_QUO_FONORD = _X_QUO_VALORD + QUO_FONORD; first year =
  VALORD);
- QUO_TOTAMM = total of the YEAR's quotas, NOT the progressive
  (allinea r.80: TOTAMM = VALORD+VALANT+VALACC) -> the detail column
  was ambiguously labelled "Totale ammortizzato": relabelled "Quote
  esercizio" with explanatory tooltip;
- the alignment PRG checks anagrafica progressives (AMM_VALORD/ANT/ACC,
  VALRES) against the LAST quota row (order annrif DESC) and realigns
  quota+fund deltas on that row (r.52-81);
- QUO_VARFIS = variazione fiscale = the ANTICIPATED quota of the year
  (gen: _X_QUO_VARFIS = _X_QUO_VALANT - anticipato is a fiscal-only,
  off-balance variation);
- QUO_IMPDIF = imposte differite = VARFIS x IRES rate
  (_X_QUO_IMPDIF = VARFIS * _W_PERC_IRES / 100, parameter from
  MENU_para_ires).
